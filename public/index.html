About Me

I am a graduate from the University of Florida with a Bachelor’s degree in Digital Arts and Sciences. Since graduation I’ve had two years of experience as a software engineer with called Mindtree Limited. During that time, I’ve specialized in working in a team environment to create new web solutions, and also to build upon and improve existing solutions, especially those based in JavaScript and C# Frameworks. I found myself in a rut growth wise, and after spending about a hear in a support based roll with no avenue for moving up, I decided to resign so that I could devote myself to furthering my education through Code Fellows. I hope to both enhance my current knowledge base and to pick up some new skills in a more formal learning environment. These are all steps that I am taking not only for my own personal development, but also so that I can use my talents to add value to any company or organization that finds interested in someone like me. Someone who values learning and using my skills to give back to others.
